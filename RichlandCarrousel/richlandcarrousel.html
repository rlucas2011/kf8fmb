Richland Carrousel Park
Posted on April 10th, 2026 • Category: Photography | Mansfield, Ohio

Built in the 1930s the Richland Carrousel is a prominent landmark in downtown Mansfield. In the 1990s an pavilion was added around the carrousel (designed by Dan Seckel) and the gift shop was built.

A few fun facts about the Carrousel (Taken from Carrousel Brochure):

• The Carrousel has 52 animals: including 30 horses and 22 menagerie figures. All were handcrafted by Carousel Works of Mansfield
• The Carousel was created by the visions of John Fernyak, Art Ritchie and Dan Jones
• The Stinson Band Organ built in Bellfontaine Ohio, provides the music for the carrousel
• The Carrousel has two speeds 3.71 MPH for those closer to the center, and 6.77 MPH for those in the outer regions

Located at 75 N. Main Street. Mansfield, Ohio 44902. In the Summer the Carrousel is open from 11:00 AM to 5:00 PM daily (Except Mondays).
For more information visit the Richland Carrousel Park Website.
